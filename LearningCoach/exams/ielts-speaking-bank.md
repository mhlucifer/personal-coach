# IELTS Speaking Bank

## Starter Set

1. Part 1: Describe a tool you use for learning.
2. Part 2: Describe a difficult technical topic you learned.
3. Part 3: Why do some people prefer self-study?
4. Follow-up: Explain one mistake you made and how you corrected it.
5. Review: Mark hesitation, grammar error, missing vocabulary, and weak structure.
