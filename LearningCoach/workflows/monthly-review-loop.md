# Monthly Review Loop

Target time: 60 to 90 minutes.

Use this at the end of each plan month. Monthly review connects daily effort to
the annual challenge line.

## Inputs

- `logs/365-progress.md`
- `logs/weekly-review.md`
- `logs/weakness.md`
- `logs/questions.md`
- this month's notes, code, scripts, and IELTS outputs

## Step 1: Score The Month

Record:

- S/A/C/0 count.
- Number of zero-day clusters.
- Outputs completed.
- Main artifact status.
- IELTS checkpoint.

## Step 2: Judge The Artifact

The monthly artifact is complete only if it has:

- a concrete file, script, table, report, or recording;
- enough evidence to be reviewed later;
- a short explanation in your own words;
- a next action.

If there is no artifact, the month is not complete even if many hours were
spent.

## Step 3: Course Anxiety Audit

List every "I am not systematic enough" moment.

For each one, decide:

- Was it a real blocker?
- Did it repeat three times?
- Did it need a course chapter, a concept card, or just a code/log example?

Only promote repeated blockers into next month's system patch.

## Step 4: Adjust Difficulty

Choose one:

- Raise challenge: if standard line was easy for two consecutive weeks.
- Hold: if standard line was hard but completed.
- Recover: if three or more zero days happened in a week.

Do not change the annual north star during monthly review.

## Step 5: Pick Next Month's Three Priorities

Choose exactly three:

1. Main technical artifact.
2. Support skill artifact.
3. IELTS checkpoint.

Everything else goes to `logs/questions.md`.

## Output Format

Append to `logs/weekly-review.md` or a monthly section:

```text
Month:
S/A/C/0:
Artifacts completed:
Main artifact quality:
Repeated blockers:
Course/system patch:
Next month's three priorities:
One thing to remove:
```
