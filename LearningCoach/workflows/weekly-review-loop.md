# Weekly Review Loop

Target time: 45-60 minutes.

## Inputs

- `logs/daily-log.md`
- `logs/weakness.md`
- `logs/questions.md`
- this week's notes and exam answers

## Steps

1. List what was studied this week.
2. Mark each item as usable, half-understood, or not understood.
3. Move repeated misses into `weakness.md`.
4. Choose only three priorities for next week.
5. Archive or defer topics that are interesting but not needed now.

## Output

Update `logs/weekly-review.md` with:

- week range
- three wins
- three weak points
- next week's three priorities
