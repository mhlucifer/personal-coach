# IELTS Roadmap

IELTS is a secondary route. Keep it practical and output-based.

## Speaking

- Build topic cards from real prompts.
- Practice short answers, then expand to Part 2 stories.
- Track weak grammar, fluency breaks, and missing vocabulary.

## Reading

- Review mistakes by question type.
- Capture why the wrong option was attractive.

## Vocabulary

- Collect words from actual IELTS material.
- Store example sentences, not isolated word lists.

## Practice Tests

- Use raw local tests as reference only.
- Upload only personal summaries and error analysis.
